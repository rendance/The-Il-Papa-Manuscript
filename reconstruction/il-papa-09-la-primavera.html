Notes

  • Note that feet in red are explicit in the text.
  • I have an alternate interpretation where the trapassini take a full measure each, and the reverenza from measure 11 moves two measures later, and the one in measure 26 is dropped - this leaves the dances as a completely repeating structure, with a 10-measure verse, 5-measure chorus.
